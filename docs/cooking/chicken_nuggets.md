# Chicken Nuggets

Tags: airfryer, low_carb, protein, snack

# Ingredients

Serving size: 8-10 pcs

- 1 Chicken Breast
- 1/4 cup bread crumbs
- Oil
- Seasonings
- 1 Egg

# Kitchen Items

- Cutting board
- Airfryer
- Knife
- Parchment paper

# Preparation

- Dice chicken breast into nugget size pieces and put in mixing bowl
- Add egg
